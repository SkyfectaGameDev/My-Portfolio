Video Editing

I've always been drawn to creating digital content, no matter the medium, and that's no different with video editing. I find making something that is very visual to be something that is really satisfying, and videos were one of the first things I even started making. The moment I got a camera, I was making videos - whether it was camping trips with my parents, making skits with my friends or simply playing games. I started making games in 2017 and making graphic art in 2015. But I've been editing videos since at least 2014. For a while I used Wondershare Filmora, but now I tend to use DaVnci Resolve.
