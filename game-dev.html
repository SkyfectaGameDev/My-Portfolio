Games Development

Since 2017, I have been dedicated towards going down the career path of a games programmer. Over that time, I have learned different software, different languages and most importantly - how to write code for different types of games, such as 2D platformers and 3D shmups. Most of my work so far has been 2D, but recently I have taught myself how to do low-poly 3D modelling in Blender, which has opened the door to begin working on 3D projects.

Below are two of my current main projects, and a showcase of all the little games I have worked on leading up to them. I have utilised multiple engines and languages - such as Unity/C#, Godot/GDScript, Game Maker Studio 2/GML, and recently I have began working with UE5/C++/Blueprints.
